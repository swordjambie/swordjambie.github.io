Oshi no Ko[a] (【推しの子】, "My Favorite Idol" or "Their Idol's Children"[3]) is a Japanese manga series written by Aka Akasaka

It has been serialized in Shueisha's Weekly Young Jump since April 2020, with its chapters collected in twelve tankōbon volumes as of July 2023.
